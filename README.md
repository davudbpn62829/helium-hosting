The company's name references its founding concept: a collection of 10 tools to ease Amazon FBA operations. The platform has since grown to over **30 specialized tools** built around a unified data infrastructure. Helium 10 sources data from Amazon's API, seller data aggregation, third-party partners, and AWS, then processes it through proprietary AI models to deliver competitive estimates and market intelligence. Its data accuracy is widely considered the highest among Amazon seller tools, a claim it supports by being the only platform with direct Amazon API integrations for certain data categories.

**What makes Helium 10 stand out in 2026:**

- **Black Box, the most comprehensive product research engine.** With access to over **450 million Amazon products** filterable by revenue, reviews, competition score, FBA fees, margin, and dozens of other parameters, Black Box helps sellers identify profitable product opportunities with a precision no competing tool matches. Advanced filters let you uncover niches other sellers miss, including filtered searches by average review rating, number of sellers, trend direction, and estimated profit margin after FBA fees.
- **Cerebro, reverse ASIN keyword intelligence.** Enter any competitor's ASIN, and Cerebro returns every keyword that the product ranks for, its search volume, ranking position, and organic vs. sponsored breakdown. This is the foundational research move for any new product launch: understand exactly what keywords drive your competitors' sales before writing a single bullet point.
- **Magnet, keyword research, and expansion.** Enter a seed keyword, and Magnet returns thousands of related search terms, including common misspellings, international variations, and trending phrases your competitors may not be targeting. Essential for building the keyword pool that feeds your listing, PPC campaigns, and A+ content.
- **Scribbles + AI Listing Builder, listing optimization.** Scribbles ensures every high-priority keyword from your research is included in your title, bullets, and description without duplicates or missed opportunities. The AI Listing Builder, introduced in 2024 and expanded in 2025, generates optimized Amazon listing copy from your keyword data in seconds, dramatically reducing the time from research to launch.

Available on Diamond and above. Sellers who move their PPC management into Adtomic consistently report reduced ACoS (Advertising Cost of Sales) and improved campaign efficiency within the first 30 days.
- **Profits Dashboard, real-time P&L.** Synced directly to your Seller Central account, the Profits Dashboard shows your real-time revenue, FBA fees, advertising costs, refunds, and net profit across every product. Unlike Seller Central's native reporting, Profits aggregates all cost categories in one view, making it the fastest way to understand your true business profitability.
- **Refund Genie + Managed Refund Service.** Refund Genie scans your FBA account for Amazon errors, lost inventory, damaged goods, and inaccurate fees and generates refund requests you can submit to Amazon. The managed version (on higher tiers) automates this process end-to-end. Amazon FBA sellers, on average, recover $2,000–$6,000/year in unclaimed reimbursements through this feature.

This cross-channel capability is unique among legacy Amazon seller tools.
